## üìä Measuring Success

Track these metrics monthly:

- **Google Search Console**
  - Impressions (how many see your listing)
  - Clicks (how many visit)
  - Average position (ranking)

- **Google Business Profile**
  - Total views
  - Search vs Maps views
  - Phone calls
  - Direction requests

- **Website Analytics**
  - Total visitors
  - Pages per session
  - Bounce rate
  - Mobile vs desktop
